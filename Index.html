What is E-Library

An E-library, short for "electronic library" or "online library," is a digital repository of resources, including books, journals, magazines, articles, multimedia content, and other educational materials, that are accessible over the internet. E-libraries provide users with the ability to access, browse, search, and read digital content from various sources and genres, often without the need to visit a physicalÂ library.
